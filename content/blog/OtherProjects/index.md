## Cupid FPS  

**Event:** BYU Spring Game Jam 2026  
**Theme:** Falling In Love  
**Engine:** Unreal Engine 5     
**Role:** Director / Gameplay Programmer  
**Project Length:** 1 week

{{< youtube id="gz3S29Q2onY" autoplay=true loop=true mute=true controls=false >}}

### Overview  
A fast-paced first-person game where the player takes on the role of Cupid, navigating a shopping mall to break up incompatible couples and correctly pair individuals under time pressure.

### Responsibilities  
- Led team planning meetings and ensured milestones were met  
- Designed and implemented core movement mechanics and level layouts  
- Iterated quickly on movement and level design inspired by *Ultrakill*  
- Integrated UI elements: toxic/healthy couple counters and a speedrun timer  
- Built the gameplay loop, allowing players to reset and attempt faster runs  
- Supported other programmers on weapon feel and combat as a resource  
- Assisted both artists and programmers to address schedule needs  

## Bonevoyage  

**Event:** BYU Fall Game Jam 2025  
**Theme:** Spooky  
**Engine:** Unreal Engine 5   
**Role:** Director / Gameplay Programmer  
**Project Length:** 1 week  

{{< carousel images="gallery/BoneVoyage/*" >}}


### Overview  
A humorous first-person visual novel where you play as a train conductor on the way to heaven. Passengers are skeletons, and you check tickets, those without are tossed out the window.

### Responsibilities  
- Led team planning meetings and ensured milestones were met  
- Designed and implemented throwing mechanics with screen shake, FX, and ribbon trails  
- Acted as technical lead, guiding programmers on gameplay systems (e.g., CSV dialogue management)  
- Directed art style and art integration, setting up scenes and incorporating assets  
- Structured project workflow and merged scripts into a cohesive system  
- Built the main gameplay loop, tracking skeleton interactions for player progress  

## Press Escape to Escape  

**Event:** BYU Summer Game Jam 2025  
**Theme:** Escape  
**Engine:** Godot   
**Role:** Solo Developer  
**Project Length:** 1 week  


{{< carousel images="gallery/EscapeToEscape/*screenshot*" >}}

### Overview  
A rhythm-based first-person platformer inspired by *Geometry Dash*, where you escape a cyber city, dodging signs, billboards, cars, and trucks while sprinting through the streets.

### Responsibilities  
- Implemented core movement mechanics: wall running, variable jumps, and fast falling  
- Whiteboxed and iterated level design using feedback to ensure fair and fun gameplay  
- Created a checkpoint system for seamless resets  
- Integrated responsive UI and music feedback  
- Built editor scripts to optimize obstacle hitboxes and background construction  

## Lego Fighter  

**Event:** CS 455 Computer Graphics: Video Game Project     
**Engine:** Unreal Engine  
**Role:** Director / Game Programmer  
**Project Length:** 2.5 weeks  

![](/gallery/LegoFighters/Screenshot.png)

### Overview  
Players control a LEGO sword fighter charging a castle to save the princess. Combat relies on simple block-and-attack mechanics, defeating all guards to win.

### Responsibilities  
- Designed core gameplay loop and combat systems  
- Implemented AI using Unreal’s Behavior Trees, with turn-based attack patterns using tokens and nav-meshes inspired by *Ghost of Tsushima*  
- Built level layouts and directed FX, animation, and art integration  
- Developed UI systems integrated into gameplay  
- Iterated on AI, combat, and level flow to ensure cohesive player experience  

## Gimbo The Great  

**Event:** BYU Fall Game Jam 2024  
**Theme:** Keep it Up  
**Engine:** Godot   
**Role:** Director / Game Programmer  
**Project Length:** 1 week  

{{< carousel images="gallery/GimboTheGreat/*screenshot*" >}}

### Overview  
A rhythm-based juggling clown simulator. Players control a clown catching objects to the beat, balancing on a ball while keeping the audience engaged. Tempo increases as juggling continues.

### Responsibilities  
- Designed core gameplay loop and juggling mechanics  
- Balanced systems including stage stability, audience interactions, and BPM progression  
- Directed and implemented all art and level setups  
- Created UI, start screen, menu navigation, and high score save system  
- Managed overall project systems to ensure cohesive gameplay and player feedback
